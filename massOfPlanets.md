
# Mass of Planets in Our Solar System from lightest to heaviest

site https://planetseducation.com/mass-of-planets-in-order/

> note that it is rounded to 0 decimal places for easy calculations

## Mercury

> mercury has the least mass of all planets in our solar system

> mass of Mercury = $3×10^{23}kg$

> since it is the least mass, it is our base mass denoted by `M`


### All Planets
- mercury = M
- mars = 2 M
- venus = 40 M
- earth = 60 M
- uranus = 900 M
- neptune = 1,000 M
- saturn = 6,000M
- jupiter = 20,000M

### Extra info 
#### Sun

> mass of sun = $2×10^{30}kg$
 that is 2,00,00,000 M
